The dirty truth about trash bins

Your bins do a dirty job — and it builds up fast

Even after the trash is gone, what’s left behind isn’t pretty. Florida heat only speeds it up. Here’s what tends to live in a can that never gets properly cleaned.

Lingering odors

Rotting residue plus warm weather creates that unmistakable trash-can smell that drifts toward your garage and front door.

Flies, maggots & pests

Sticky build-up invites flies, maggots, and other pests — along with the bacteria and grime that come with them.

A hose won’t cut it

A quick rinse with the garden hose just pushes the mess around — it doesn’t truly clean or deodorize the inside of the bin.

Right next to home

These cans sit by your driveway, garage, pets, and family — so the grime is a lot closer to daily life than it should be.

Trash bin cleaning FAQs

Everything you need to know about curbside garbage can cleaning with Happy Pails.

How does Happy Pails clean my bins?
We wash, sanitize, and deodorize your bins right at the curb after your trash is picked up. The process is designed to lift away grime and residue and leave your garbage cans cleaner and fresher, helping reduce odors.
Do I need to be home?
Nope! As long as your empty bins are out and accessible at the curb, you don’t need to be home. Come back to clean, fresh bins on your schedule.
When should I leave my bins out?
Leave your empty bins at the curb after your regular trash pickup so they’re ready for cleaning. We’ll confirm timing details with you when you schedule.
How often should trash bins be cleaned?
It depends on your household and the Florida heat, but many customers like a recurring schedule to keep odors and build-up from coming back. Others prefer a one-time refresh as needed — we’re happy to help you choose.
Can I schedule recurring service?
Yes — recurring bin cleaning is our most popular option, and recurring customers currently get 50% off. Just call or text 352-644-0237 to set it up.
Do you clean residential garbage cans?
Absolutely. Residential trash bin cleaning is what we do. We also offer service for HOAs, property managers, and rental properties.
How do I sign up?
Use the contact form below, or simply call or text us. We’ll get you scheduled and answer any questions about service and pricing.
Can I call or text?
